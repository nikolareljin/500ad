# Leader Portrait Credits

This folder includes portrait-style leader images prepared as `1024x1024` JPEGs for in-game use.

The following files were sourced from Wikimedia Commons depictions (historical portraits, busts, miniatures, or reconstructions where available), then cropped/resized for game assets.

## Processing Notes

- Images were cropped and resized to `1024x1024`
- Output format: `JPEG`
- Minor framing adjustments were applied to center faces/figures for portrait presentation
